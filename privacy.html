Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

Release Workshop Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") operates Control Path, a Git-native, type-safe feature flag solution. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website, open source software, and related services.

Open Source Software

Control Path is open source software. When you use Control Path:

  • No data collection: Control Path runs entirely locally in your environment. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data from your use of the software.
  • No telemetry: The software does not include any telemetry, analytics, or tracking mechanisms.
  • Your data stays local: All feature flag definitions, evaluations, and data remain in your repository and infrastructure.

Website Usage

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Log data: Standard web server logs including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This data is used for security and website operation purposes.
  • Cookies: We may use essential cookies for website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

GitHub and Open Source Contributions

If you contribute to Control Path on GitHub:

  • Public information: Contributions, issues, pull requests, and discussions are public and subject to GitHub's privacy policy.
  • No additional collection: We do not collect additional personal information beyond what is publicly available on GitHub.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to processing of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
